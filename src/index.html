Tigray Region

Axum || አክሱም

This fascinating town was the centre of the Axumite Empire, one of the most important and technologically advanced civilisations of its time and a major force in world trade between the 1st and 7th centuries AD.

Lasta Mountains

Lalibela || ላሊበላ

The isolated town of Lalibela, situated high up in the Lasta Mountains, is famous throughout the world for its incredible rock-hewn churches. At 2,630 m and perched among wild mountains, the setting is fantastic. .

Afar Region

Danakil || ዳናኪል

The Danakil Depression is found in Northern Ethiopia, close to the border with Eritrea. With parts of Danakil 100 metres below sea level, this is one of the lowest places on Earth and also one of the hottest in average yearly temperature.

Addis Ababa

Unity||አንድነት ፓርክ

The Unity Park of Ethiopia is one of the most modern and multi-faceted tourist attractions in the country. The Ethiopian Unity Park is in the compound of the National Grand Palace, which is at Arat kilo in Addis Ababa.

Some Facts
aboutEthiopia

More than 70% of Africa's mountains are found in Ethiopia.

Lake Tana is the source of the Blue Nile River, which meets the White Nile River in Sudan to form the Great Nile River

The Great Rift Valley, cuts through Ethiopia from the northeast to the south of the country

Ethiopia is the only country in Africa with its own unique script.

Ethiopia remains one of the only nations in Africa never to be colonized.

There are 31 endemic mammal species found in Ethiopia.

Ethiopia is the home of the Black Jews, known as the Falashas, or Beta Israel..

There are 175 species of fish found in Ethiopia, 40 of which are endemic..

There are 924 species of birds found in Ethiopia, making Ethiopia a bird-watchers paradise. Of these, 23 species are endemic to Ethiopia.

Coffee, one of the world's most popular beverages, was discovered in Ethiopia, in the region of Kaffa..

Ethiopia is home to 9 UNESCO World Heritage sites, more than any other country in Africa.
